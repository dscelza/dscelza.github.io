Hi, I'm Dylan!

I'm a graduate from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Minor in Applied Statistics. I’m interested in the intersection between business and technology ranging from fintech to biotech.

Before college, I started exploring mobile application development to expand on my entrepreneurial initiatives. Through the founding of CoconutStorm.com in 2010, I launched several mobile applications for Windows products. Coconut Storm’s leading application has received multiple Windows Store features by Microsoft and accumulated over 250,000 downloads.

In the summer of 2016, I gained invaluable experience in the biotechnology industry at Amgen in Thousand Oaks, CA. As an intern within their cybersecurity unit of Information Systems, I balanced projects centering on software engineering and internal business development. Beginning the following summer, I joined the ambitious team at Jane Street’s New York office in a full-time position focused on the financial markets.
